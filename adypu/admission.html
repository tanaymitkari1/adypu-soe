How To Apply

Easy & Student Friendly

STEP 1: Check The Eligibility

The eligibility for each of the program offered by the University is different and is based on the program curriculum. Before applying, check the eligibility for that program Click Here

STEP 2: Application Process

The application process has two stages:.

At the Stage One, The aspiring candidate registers online and appears for ACET (ADYPU Common Entrance Test).

For more information on ACET and ACET registration Click Here

The application process has two stages:

At Stage One, The aspiring candidate registers online and appears for ACET online.

The candidates are short-listed based on their performance in the ACET. Our Academic Counselor will call the shortlisted candidate within 3 working days for scheduling the Personal Interview.

At Stage two, candidates are invited to the ADYPU campus for a Counseling Session and a personal interview. For MBA programs, candidates have to appear for Group Discussion and for Design programs candidates are required to give a Studio test. The students have to bring along with them the Statement of Purpose (SOP), certificates of extracurricular activities if any.

It is essential to attend the Second Stage for each student who qualified through the ACET. The student selection is based on the cumulative performance of students in both the first and the second rounds.



STEP 3: Admission Offer

After the personal interview is conducted, the prospective student is informed about the admission decision. If the admission decision is positive, an admission offer letter is issued. If the admission decision is negative, the prospective student is informed about the deficiencies and is given time to remedy the deficiencies if deficiencies are of remedial nature.

STEP 4: Acceptance of Admission Form

ADYPU and the student form a contractual relationship when a student is enrolled at ADYPU. What follows are the terms of that contract in which the students accept to govern the mutual relationship, along with statutes, and with the regulations and rules of ADYPU. On being offered an admission to a course at ADYPU, the student has to sign an “Acceptance of Admission Form”.

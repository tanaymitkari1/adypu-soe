B.Tech Mechanical Engineering


Duration :4 Years

Eligibility : XII’th in Science.

Specializations in :

Mechanical Design

Automobile Engineering


Machine is an integral part of human life today. In today’s modern world, use of machine is ubiquitous, from agriculture to construction to aeronautics, from kitchens to factories, from hospitals to space, from crèche to cemetery; everywhere in the world life is impossible without mechanical gadgets.

Mechanical engineering is the pivotal and mainstream area of engineering and technology. Mechanical engineers conduct research, design and develop prototypes, carry out testing, and build devices. They help build a nation’s infrastructure to create modern societies. The problems related to safety, energy insufficiency, speedy transportation, lowering costs of myriads of manufacturing processes, energy efficient and sustainable machines have always dreaded nations and brought about cataclysmic changes in their socio-economic and political lives. Mechanical engineers provide solutions to innumerable problems and contribute to bring in stability in a nation’s life.

Our undergraduate program in BTech Mechanical Engineering endows you with the knowledge of several fundamental concepts related to Mechanical Engineering. Students receive multiple opportunities to learn in many ways, which include enriching class-room instruction by experienced professors, laboratory experiments, industry visits, workshop by industry professionals and real-time industry internships. At ADYPU, the students get to learn the concepts not only by the most efficient professors, but also by industry professionals and experts and international faculties; and above all, what students learn is not just within the four-walls of the school but they actually work on real-industry concepts.

The Program

ADYPU School of Engineering offers you a unique program in B Tech Mechanical Engineering designed to transform you into an engineer of global standards. The program offers time-tested, standards-based curriculum, based on international framework specified by Accreditation Board of Engineering and Technology (ABET, USA). Our B Tech Mechanical Engineering program has been designed to deliver multiple learning outcomes. A salient few of them are:


  • An ability to apply knowledge of mechanics, mathematics and design to create sustainable solutions to problems.
  • An ability to design prototypes, conduct experiments, and finalize the prototypes.
  • An ability to design a system, process or device to meet the desired needs in the realistic constrains.
  • An ability to use the techniques, skills, and modern engineering tools necessary for engineering practice.
  • An ability to function on multidisciplinary teams.

Students develop competence in engineering tools, design of individual and multicomponent systems, modeling, mechanical systems along with communication skills and computer skills required for mechanical engineering.

Mechanical Design is emphasized throughout the curriculum. This is the key differentiator of ADYPU Mechanical Engineering program. Design is integrated throughout the four-year course starting with two foundation design courses in first year. Design projects in each semester from s econd year onwards increase in scope with increasing complexity. The design component introduces students to formal design methods, application of CAD systems, and the professional aspects of engineering. The design project imbibes in the student professional aspects of engineering including teamwork, oral and written communications. The final year project builds on the previous year work and leads to a major design project of unique significance.

Students are imparted valuable computer experience right from first year itself. Students begin with programming language, CAD software and continue using computer applications throughout the curriculum. On the way, they learn mathematical modeling software, commercial software packages for CAD, spreadsheets, finite element analysis, and many such applications.

The school environment is open, relaxed and interactive so as to enable exchange of new ideas and gain a wealth of knowledge. The school is a perfect place for connections, knowledge and tools that you would need to transform yourself into an engineer of a global caliber and successfully embark on a solid career path.


Careers

By the time students graduate they acquire technical skills required for immediate entry into industry. B Tech Mechanical Engineers fit into many jobs. They can get jobs in many sectors like Tool Manufacturing, Product Design, Automobile Engineering, Aerospace, Energy, Agricultural Technology, Technical Education and many more. Mechanical engineers are employed in almost every sector in an economy as they are preferred for their design, mathematical and analytical skills.
